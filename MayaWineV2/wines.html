Carta de la Abuela

Cabernet Sauvignon Merlot

Semi-Sweet

Chile | 1.5 L

A warm and long summer permits us to harvest this vine-stock variety in April. It is a ruby red color wine with an aroma of ripe plums, cherries and elegant touches of toasted wood. At taste, it presents soft tannins, with a full body and a pleasant aftertaste. We suggest you serve at 18 degrees Celsius, with pasta and a variety of cheeses.
